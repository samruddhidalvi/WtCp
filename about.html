About Bharat Hardware Stores

Your trusted partner in home improvement and hardware solutions since 1980.

Our Story

Bharat Hardware Stores began as a humble venture by Ram Dalvi, who had a passion for providing quality tools and materials to the community. With his dedication and hard work, he laid the foundation for what would become a trusted name in the hardware industry.

As the years passed, Sachin Dalvi and Vrushali Dalvi took over the business, infusing it with fresh ideas and expanding our range of products. Together, they ensured that Bharat Hardware Stores remained committed to excellence, serving generations of customers with the same integrity and care that my grandfather instilled in us.

We started with just a few basic tools and have since expanded to offer a wide range of products including plumbing supplies, electrical tools, household hardware, and more. Our team is dedicated to helping you find exactly what you need for your projects.
